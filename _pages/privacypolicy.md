# Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Effective Date: June 16, 2026
Thank you for using upliNK.

Information We Collect

NONE! 

upliNK does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

Specifically, the app:
- Does not require user accounts.
- Does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal information.
- Does not track user activity.
- Does not use analytics services.
- Does not use advertising networks.
- Does not collect device identifiers.
- Does not store user-generated content on external servers.

Third-Party Services
upliNK does not use third-party services that collect user information.

Children's Privacy
Because no personal information is collected, upliNK does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 or any other age.

Data Security
Since no user data is collected or stored, there is no personal information maintained by the app. All user information is stored in the user's private CoreData environment on the user's device. 

Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on the application's distribution page, and the updated effective date will be revised accordingly.
